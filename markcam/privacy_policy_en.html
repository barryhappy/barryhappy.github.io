Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2024

Thank you for using MarkCam! This Privacy Policy is designed to inform you about how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before using our application.

Information Collection and Use

MarkCam is a camera application that allows users to take photos and add watermarks. To provide these features, we may collect the following information:

  • Camera permission: For the core functionality of taking photos.
  • Storage permission: To save photos to your device.
  • Location information (optional): If you allow, we add location information to watermarks.

How We Use Collected Information

All information we collect is used solely to provide and improve MarkCam's features. Specifically:

  • Camera functionality is used to take photos
  • Storage permission is used to save photos to your device
  • Location information (if you allow) is only used to display location markers in watermarks

All data processing takes place on your device, and we do not upload your personal data to servers.

Data Storage and Sharing

Photos taken with MarkCam are saved in your device's internal storage, which you can access at any time through your device's gallery application. We do not share your personal information with any third parties, unless:

  • We have your explicit permission
  • We are required to do so by law

Data Security

We value your privacy and data security. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure. However, please note that no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us, and we will take steps to remove that information from our servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please do not hesitate to contact us through the "Feedback" feature in the app or by sending an email to support@markcam.app.
